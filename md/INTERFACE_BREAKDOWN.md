# 🖥️ Role-Based Interface Breakdown

This document provides a detailed overview of the pages and features available to each user role in the system.

---

## 🔑 Admin (Full Access)
The Admin has 100% visibility and control over the platform.

### 🏛️ Navigation Tabs:
1.  **Dashboard**: Global business analytics, revenue charts, and overall shop performance.
2.  **Orders**: Manage the full lifecycle of every order. Can override any status or detail.
3.  **Quotations**: Review all price estimates and convert them to formal orders.
4.  **Clients**: Complete CRM access. Manage client profiles, contact info, and history.
5.  **Products**: Manage the shop's catalog (add/edit products, categories, and SKUs).
6.  **Production**: Oversee the production floor and jump in to help when needed.
7.  **Team**: **[Admin Only]** Create new user accounts, assign roles, and manage employee access.
8.  **Settings**: **[Admin Only]**
    *   **General**: Shop name, logo, and core configurations.
    *   **Status Workflow**: Customize the order pipeline and status colors.
    *   **Pricing Tiers**: Configure margins, tax rates, and currency settings.

---

## 💼 Sales (Growth & CRM)
Sales focuses on customer acquisition and initiating the order process.

### 🏛️ Navigation Tabs:
1.  **Dashboard**: Personal sales performance and recent customer activity.
2.  **Orders**: Create new orders and track existing ones for their clients.
3.  **Quotations**: Main workspace. Creating, editing, and converting price quotes.
4.  **Clients**: Access to client database to manage communication and history.
5.  **Products**: View catalog to provide accurate quotes to customers.

---

## 🎨 Designer (Visual Assets)
The Designer's interface is streamlined to focus only on creative assets.

### 🏛️ Navigation Tabs:
1.  **Orders (Design Mode)**: A filtered view showing only orders that require design work.
    *   **Features**: Upload mockups, download client references, and finalize "Print-Ready" files.
2.  **Products**: View product specifications to ensure design dimensions are correct.

---

## ⚙️ Production (Factory Floor)
The Production team has a specialized "Task-Oriented" view optimized for speed.

### 🏛️ Navigation Tabs:
1.  **Production Queue**: The primary workspace.
    *   **Features**: Large cards with clear status badges, delivery deadlines, notes, and one-click "Start Job" or "Mark as Ready" buttons.
2.  **Orders**: View all orders to see context or history.
3.  **Products**: Check product details and inventory references.

---

## 💰 Accountant (Finance)
The Accountant monitors the financial health without interfering in the daily production.

### 🏛️ Navigation Tabs:
1.  **Dashboard**: Financial-heavy view focusing on revenue and payment statuses.
2.  **Orders**: Review invoice totals and payment balances.
3.  **Clients**: Manage credit limits, debts, and billing information.
4.  **Products**: Review base costs and pricing strategies.
